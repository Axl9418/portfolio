The District Factory outlet

Official Website

Website developed for the District Factory Outlet, which is a souvenir store located in Downtown, Vancouver, BC.

It arises from the need to attract more local clients, since due to the COVID-19, tourists are scarce, who are the main market for this business.

This website was developed using HTML5, JavaScript, PHP, Bootstrap, Ajax, Jquery, and CSS. Ensuring that it meets industry standards and is compatible with all browsers, devices, and operating systems.

You can visit this website at: https://thedistrictfactoryoutlet.com
